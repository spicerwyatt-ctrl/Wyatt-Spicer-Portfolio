Here's a bit about what I do, what I make, and how I approach design across different mediums.

Bio

I am a multidisciplinary designer studying Arts, Technology, and Emerging Communication at UT Dallas, graduating in December 2025. My work sits at the intersection of UX design, 3D modeling, web development, and multimedia storytelling. I'm drawn to projects that blend functional design with playful, unexpected ideas, whether that's building interactive installations, designing apps, creating character-driven 3D objects, or developing culture-jamming experiences that challenge how communities communicate.

Across every medium, my practice is driven by curiosity, experimentation, and a desire to make complex systems feel intuitive and engaging. I'm currently based in Dallas and interested in roles or collaborations involving UX/UI design, product design, digital fabrication, and creative technology.
