Disciplines

This portfolio brings together four parts of my creative practice; UX, web design, 3D fabrication, and audio/visual work. These pieces reflect how I approach problem-solving, experimentation, and visual storytelling across different mediums.

UX Case Studies

Clear, user-focused design work built through research, prototyping, and iterative testing. My UX projects include interview-driven insights, affinity mapping, personas, wireframes, and high-fidelity prototypes that solve real problems through thoughtful interaction design.

3D & Fabrication

Digital fabrication and object design created using Rhino, Grasshopper, KeyShot, and 3D printing workflows. This includes functional tools, themed objects, and experimental forms, combining precision modeling with playful concepts and polished physical outputs.

Web Projects

Custom-coded sites that blend design, branding, and storytelling. These projects explore layout, typography, interaction, and responsive structure through HTML/CSS builds, campaign sites, and experimental web concepts.

Art, Audio & Visual

Creative work across multiple mediums, including audio-reactive visualizers built in TouchDesigner, short film projects, and digital paintings. These pieces highlight experimentation with sound, motion, narrative, and visual style outside traditional UX.
